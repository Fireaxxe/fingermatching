Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 28, 2025

Thank you for playing Finger Matching. Your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how we handle any data when you use our iOS or iPadOS app.

1. No Personal Data Collection

Finger Matching runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

2. How the App Works

  • All touch tracking and logic execute locally on your device.
  • No touch positions, usage statistics, or analytics are uploaded or transmitted.
  • Sound and haptic feedback are generated in-app—no external servers are involved.

3. No Third-Party Trackers

We do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or SDKs that collect user data.

4. Sound & Haptic Resources

Audio files (touchspawn.mp3, match.mp3, etc.) and haptic patterns remain bundled within the app. No usage or performance metrics are sent externally.

5. Security & Control

You remain in control at all times. The app does not store any logs or recordings of your interactions.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected here with a new “Effective Date.”

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please reach out to us at kotsiosfireaxe@gmail.com.
